# Landing Page Override (Lumina)
Specific design rules for the marketing site.

## Hero Section
- **Pattern:** Hero-Centric (Min-height 100vh)
- **Headline:** Bold Outfit (size: clamp 2.5rem, 5vw, 4rem)
- **Primary CTA:** Pulsing Orange Button `#F97316`
- **Background:** Subtle animated Aurora gradient (Indigo → Light Blue)

## Feature Grid
- **Layout:** Bento Box Grid (Asymmetric)
- **Card Style:** Mix of 1x1, 2x1, and 2x2 cards.
- **Glassmorphism:** Apply backdrop blur (8px) to cards on gradient backgrounds.

## Trust Signals
- **Social Proof:** Grayscale logo grid for university logos.
- **Micro-copy:** Focus on "Study Clarity" and "Nigerian University Context".
